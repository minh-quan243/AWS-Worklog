### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is generally friendly and open. Team members at FCJ are usually willing to provide support when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace feels comfortable and helps me stay focused. However, due to the large number of interns, opportunities to work onsite are limited, and most of the work is done remotely.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Management**  
The mentor provided enthusiastic support throughout the project and helped address questions. In addition, workshops and events also contributed valuable knowledge.

**3. Relevance to Academic Major**  
Before the internship, I did not have much knowledge about AWS and cloud services. However, during the internship, I had the opportunity to explore and better understand this field, as well as recognize the importance of cloud computing. The project I participated in involved incident response automation and forensic analysis on AWS, which leans toward Information Security. Although it does not fully align with my academic program, it still provided valuable experience.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout the internship, I had the opportunity to develop skills such as working with AWS services, using Python in projects, and improving collaboration and support within the team—especially in a remote working environment.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The work culture is positive, with mutual respect among team members and a serious but not overly stressful atmosphere. Mentors are young and friendly, making communication easy. Team members are proactive, committed to completing tasks, and supportive of one another, especially during the final stage of the project.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program supports flexible and remote working. In addition, the company introduced AWS Study Group and organized knowledge-sharing sessions, providing useful learning direction.

### Additional Questions

- **What are you most satisfied with during the internship?**  
  **Answer:** I had the opportunity to learn how to use AWS services and better understand how to configure appropriate policies when building systems.

- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
  **Answer:** Having more onsite working space would be more convenient, although I understand it may be difficult to implement. Additionally, the scope of the midterm assessment was broader than expected. If communicated more clearly in advance, we could have prepared better.

- **Would you recommend this internship to your friends? Why?**  
  **Answer:** I would still recommend it, but it may be more suitable for those who already have a stable working group and are self-disciplined in remote environments. Some OJT interns may face challenges when assigned to randomly formed teams.
