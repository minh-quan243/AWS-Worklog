### Internship Evaluation 

During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam** from **11/01/2026** to **17/04/2026**, I had the opportunity to experience a real working environment while applying and reinforcing the knowledge I had learned at university.  

During this time, I contributed to and supported my team in building a **speech-to-text system integrated with a chatbot on AWS**. Through this experience, I was able to gradually improve several skills, including **working with AWS and cloud services, learning and using Python for AWS Lambda, and collaborating with team members in a remote working environment**.  

In terms of work attitude, I consistently tried to complete assigned tasks to the best of my ability and support my teammates whenever possible. I also maintained regular communication with the team and followed general working guidelines, even while working remotely.

### Areas for Improvement

* Although I adapted to remote work, my productivity was sometimes not as consistent as when working onsite  
* I believe I could have been more proactive in communicating with my mentor and other FCJ members  
* I need to improve task distribution within the team, as I occasionally took on too much work myself. Fortunately, my teammates were proactive and supported me, encouraging better task delegation
