### Internship Evaluation

During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam** from **January 11, 2026** to **April 17, 2026**, I had the opportunity to experience a real working environment while applying and reinforcing the knowledge I learned at university.  

During this period, I participated in and supported my team in building a **speech-to-text system integrated with a chatbot on AWS**. Through this experience, I gradually improved several skills such as **working with AWS and cloud services, learning and using Python for AWS Lambda, as well as collaborating and supporting the team in a remote working environment**.  

Regarding my work attitude, I always tried to complete assigned tasks within my capabilities and support team members when needed. At the same time, I maintained regular communication with the team and followed general guidelines, even though most of the work was done remotely.

### Areas for Improvement

* Although I have adapted to remote work, my productivity is sometimes less stable compared to working onsite.  
* I believe I need to be more proactive in communicating with my mentor and FCJ team members.  
* I should improve task management and workload distribution, as I sometimes take on too many tasks at once. Fortunately, my teammates were proactive in supporting me and encouraged me to manage my workload more effectively.
